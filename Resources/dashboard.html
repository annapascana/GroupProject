Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a collaboration?

Click on any collaboration card to navigate to that specific page and follow the instructions to join.

How do I create my own collaboration?

Navigate to the specific collaboration type page (e.g., Travel, Study Group) and look for the "Create" button.

How do I update my profile?

Click on your name in the sidebar to edit your profile information.
